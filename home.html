The FIFA Women's World Cup is a professional association football tournament contested by senior women's national football teams, organized by FIFA. The tournament, held every four years and one year after the men's World Cup, was first played in 1991 in China The tournament is contested with eight round-robin groups followed by a knockout round for 16 teams.

The 2023 FIFA Women's World Cup is the ninth edition of the FIFA Women's World Cup The tournament, which took place from 20 July to 20 August 2023, is jointly hosted by Australia and New Zealand It is the first FIFA Women's World Cup with more than one host nation, as well as the first World Cup to be held across multiple confederations, as Australia is in the Asian confederation, while New Zealand is in the Oceanian confederation.this tournament is the first to feature the expanded format of 32 teams from the previous 24

The hosting of the 2023 FIFA Women's World Cup by Australia and New Zealand was widely praised and was the most attended edition of the competition ever held. The competition has been described by some as the best Women's World Cup in history.
